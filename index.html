How to install in Gmail

  1. Click Download Image above to save your signature as a PNG.
  2. Open Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings.
  3. Scroll to the Signature section and click Create new.
  4. In the signature editor, click the Insert image icon (mountain/sun icon in the toolbar).
  5. Choose Upload and select the PNG you just downloaded.
  6. Click the inserted image, then click Large in the size options that appear below it.
  7. Scroll down to Signature defaults and set both For new emails use and On reply/forward use to Punch Signature.
  8. Scroll down and click Save Changes.
